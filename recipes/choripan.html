Choripan

Description

Chorip√°n is a classic Argentine street food, consisting of a grilled chorizo sausage served in crusty bread and often topped with chimichurri sauce.

Ingredients

  • 4 chorizo sausages
  • 4 baguette-style rolls
  • Chimichurri sauce to taste

Steps

  1. Grill the chorizo sausages until cooked through.
  2. Slightly toast the rolls on the grill.
  3. Place the sausages in the rolls and top with chimichurri sauce.
  4. Serve hot.
